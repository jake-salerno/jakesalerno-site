About

At the intersection of blockchain and AI

Jake Salerno is the VP of Go-to-Market at 0G Labs, where he built and leads the team responsible for ecosystem growth, partnerships, and go-to-market strategy for the first decentralized AI operating system.

Since joining 0G in 2024, Jake has helped scale the ecosystem to over 350 integrations, shaping how AI and Web3 connect across modular infrastructure. Under his leadership, 0G launched its Aristotle mainnet with 100+ partners activated on day one.

Before 0G, Jake spent three years at Chainlink Labs leading go-to-market for data products that contributed to over $1 trillion in Total Value Secured across the Web3 ecosystem. He's been building in crypto since 2017, when he co-founded the Miami University Blockchain Club — one of the first university-level blockchain organizations in the Midwest.

Jake is a Miami University alum with degrees in Architecture and Entrepreneurship from the Farmer School of Business. He's based in Austin, Texas.
